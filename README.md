# Zomato Gold Retention Strategy — Product Case Study

**How do you keep members after month 3?** Zomato Gold loses **45% of its members at the first renewal decision**. This case study diagnoses why — with primary research — and proposes a prioritized, ecosystem-led retention strategy.

📂 **[Full deck (PDF)](deck/Zomato_Gold_Retention_Strategy.pdf)** · 16 slides · [Editable PPTX](deck/Zomato_Gold_Retention_Strategy.pptx)

---

## TL;DR

> Gold is being used as a **coupon**, not a **membership**. Members re-shop every order because nothing makes staying obviously better. The fix isn't deeper discounts — it's extending Gold across the ecosystem Zomato already owns (Blinkit, District), so the membership earns its renewal weekly instead of asking for trust quarterly.

## The problem

- **45%** of Gold members do not renew after month 3 — top stated reason: *"didn't order enough to justify it"*
- Perceived value collapses in **month 2**, long before the renewal decision
- **65%** of members touch delivery perks only; Blinkit / District / dining benefits go unseen

## Research

| Method | What we did |
|---|---|
| **Primary survey** | 71 Gold users — active, churned & expired — across Delhi NCR |
| **Personas** | 4 archetypes clustered by frequency, spend & motivation |
| **Journey mapping** | 11 touchpoints scored for drop-off risk; checkout = critical leak |
| **Benchmarking** | Swiggy One, EatSure Elite, ONDC, QSR loyalty programs |

**Key survey signals:** 74% of orders under ₹500 (solo meals dominate) · 71% order weekly+ · 52% run Swiggy side-by-side (zero lock-in) · month-2 = sharpest value drop.

## Four bets (from eight surfaced problems)

| # | Problem | Bet |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Swiggy One out-values Gold with food + grocery bundling | **Gold Everywhere** — one membership across Zomato, Blinkit & District |
| 2 | Gold members queue behind free users at support | **Gold Priority Support** — human in 60 seconds, auto-credit on failures |
| 3 | 40–50 min delivery breaks the 60-min office lunch | **Gold Express** — 30–35 min promise, on-time-or-credit |
| 4 | Choice overload burns the lunch break | **Gold AI Concierge** — one-tap pick from your history, budget & time window |

## Prioritization — RICE

| Bet | Reach /10 | Impact /3 | Confidence | Effort (pm) | **Score** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Gold Everywhere** | 9 | 3.0 | 90% | 3 | **8.1** 🥇 |
| Priority Support | 4 | 2.0 | 90% | 2 | 3.6 |
| Gold Express | 6 | 2.5 | 70% | 6 | 1.75 |
| AI Concierge | 5 | 2.0 | 60% | 7 | 0.86 |

**Why the winner wins:** Blinkit and District are already Zomato-owned. Swiggy must *operate* Instamart to fund its bundle; Zomato only has to flip a membership flag. **Integration, not construction.**

## Impact targets

- D90 Gold renewal: **18% → 38%** (north star)
- 3-month churn: **45% → <30%**
- Orders / member / month: **4 → 4.4**
- Multi-vertical usage: **35% → 60%**

Rollout: **Phase 1 (Q3)** Gold Everywhere + Priority Support → **Phase 2 (Q4)** Gold Express pilot in 3 metros → **Phase 3 (Q1+)** AI Concierge (rule-based "usuals" rail first, ML after 35% acceptance).
